About Guruji

🕉️

Guruji — the divine light of Dugri, Ludhiana — blessed millions of devotees with his unconditional love, grace, and spiritual guidance. His presence alone was enough to transform lives forever.

Born in Dugri, Ludhiana, Guruji's satsangs were filled with divine energy, langar, bhajans, and his boundless grace. He never distinguished between rich or poor, Hindu, Sikh, or Muslim — all were equal in his shelter. His blessings continue to flow through every devotee who remembers him with a pure heart. Jai Guruji 🙏 Shukrana Guruji.
